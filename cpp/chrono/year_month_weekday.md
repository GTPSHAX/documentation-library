class year_month_weekday;
The class `year_month_weekday` represents the n weekday of a specific year and month. It is a field-based time point, with a resolution of `std::chrono::days`. `std::chrono::years`- and `std::chrono::months`-oriented arithmetic are supported directly. An implicit conversion to and from `std::chrono::sys_days` allows `std::chrono::days`-oriented arithmetic to be performed efficiently.
`year_month_weekday` is a *TriviallyCopyable* *StandardLayoutType*.
